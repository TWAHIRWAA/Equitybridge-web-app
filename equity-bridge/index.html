How "Boys to Men" is shaping Rwanda’s
next generation of leaders

Boys to Men," translating to "From Boyhood to Manhood," is a transformative initiative launched by Equity Bridge, a non-profit organization founded in 2021. The program focuses on community welfare, quality education, and environmental sustainability.

Numa Alain, the visionary behind "Boys to Men," started the program emerged as a divine calling. He explained: "God revealed to me the importance of guiding young boys, who face overwhelming modern-day challenges that could mislead them. Without intervention, building future leaders for Rwanda will become increasingly difficult. Proverbs 22:6 guided this mission."
